Understanding Online Hotel Reviews on Facebook Using Structural Topic Model

Role: Third Author

This research explores how users express opinions in online hotel reviews on Facebook using Structural Topic Modeling (STM). The study aims to uncover latent topics, sentiment patterns, and key drivers influencing customer satisfaction.

My Contributions:

  • Data preprocessing and cleaning of large-scale social media text data.
  • Assisted in topic modeling implementation using statistical tools.
  • Interpreted model outputs and translated findings into business insights.

Key Findings:

  • Identified core themes such as service quality, location, and pricing perception.
  • Revealed how emotional tone affects perceived credibility of reviews.
  • Demonstrated the value of social media data in hospitality decision-making.
